Pre-Cast & Concrete Remedial Case Study

Scaling High-Quality Leads in Melbourne Construction Industry

📌 Project Overview

A Melbourne-based Pre-Cast & Concrete Remedial specialist approached us to generate consistent, high-quality leads from builders, contractors, and property developers. The goal was to build a scalable lead generation system using Google Ads within a fixed monthly budget.

🎯 Objectives

Generate qualified construction leads
Reduce cost per lead
Target high-intent commercial clients
Improve conversion rate

⚠️ Challenges

  • Highly competitive construction niche in Melbourne
  • Low search volume but high CPC keywords
  • Unqualified inquiries from residential users
  • No prior structured ad funnel

🚀 Strategy Implemented

Search Campaigns

Focused on high-intent keywords like “concrete repair contractors Melbourne”.

Landing Page Optimization

Created service-specific pages with strong CTAs and trust signals.

Audience Filtering

Excluded residential traffic and targeted B2B segments.

Conversion Tracking

Implemented call + form tracking via GTM & GA4.
